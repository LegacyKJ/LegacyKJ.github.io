Services

Whether you are looking for a little advice that your plans are going in the right direction or want someone to preserve your materials for you, I am happy to help!

All of my services are customized to your unique materials and their needs. Below, you can learn more about my approach to working with clients as well as what kinds of materials I can help you with.

If you have something in mind but don't see it mentioned here, please send me a message. I am more than happy to have a conversation about what options may exist or connect you to other resources.

Approach

My goal is to provide varying levels of service to meet individuals and institutions where they are at. To do this, I have three different levels of service that range from a consultation to me completing the work on your behalf.

All services begin with a free initial consultation that is conducted over a phone or video call. This is a chance for me to get to know you and learn more about your project. The following are some of the questions I will ask during our first call. In the sections that follow, you can learn more about the options that are available for each.

  • What level of help can I provide for you?
  • What type of services do you need?
  • What type of material(s) do you have?
  • Where will this work need to be done? 
  • What is your goal? What end result are you looking for?

Level of Service Options

I need...

 A Nudge

One-on-one consultation time beyond the initial consultation. This is to serve those who want some guidance, additional resources, etc. to ensure you are on the right track.

A Plan

More involved than "a nudge", this option provides one-on-one consultations that result in an individualized work plan for your specific project. I can also provide training for skills needed in the work plan for you to be able to complete the plan successfully.

A Hand

A step above "a plan", this option provides one-on-one consultations where I will develop a plan of work that I will carry out to complete a project for you. 

* Most projects are completed at my studio, located in Potsdam, NY. There are options for me to come onsite if desired, depending on the service requested.

Service Options

Archival Education

Provide archival instruction for individuals and institutions about best practices for physical and digital preservation.

  • One-on-one workshops
  • Small group workshops
  • Remote instruction

Collections Care

Perform and/or advise on collection care tasks for physical and digital materials including: 

  • Assessment & Selection
  • Collection & Organization
  • Preservation
  • Accessibility

Digitization

Digitally capture physical materials for preservation, ease of use, and reuse including the following formats:

  • Manuscript Materials
  • Printed Materials
  • Images (Printed and Negatives)
  • Audio & Video Recordings (Select Formats)

Materials

Reflective

This includes paper based material(s) that light bounces off for us to view it. Some items include:

  • Photographs
  • Books
  • Drawings
  • Maps
  • Diaries/Journals
  • Papers/Records
  • Cased Images
  • Printed Material(s)
  • Letters/Postcards
  • Scrapbooks/Albums
  • Etc.

Transparent

Light passes through these material(s) for us to view, including:

  • Film Based Negatives (various sizes)
  • Glass Plate Negatives
  • 35mm slides

Audio/Video (A/V)

These materials can't be viewed without some sort of playback device. Currently, I can accommodate:

  • VHS Tapes
  • 8mm Film (no audio)
  • Super8 Film (no audio)
  • Cassette Tapes
