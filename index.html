 Contact Me
Hi! My name is Katelyn. I founded Something Treasured to provide professional services in support of the preservation of community memory for individuals and small institutions through archival education, collections care, and digitization. 

I know that your materials are unique and that they deserve to be treated as such. I provide guidance and create work plans to accommodate YOUR materials specific needs. Each project is evaluated on an individual basis with the priority being to care for the original materials in line with current best practices. 

Whether you are looking for someone to talk to for guidance to care for materials yourself or have work that you would like me to complete for you, I am here to help.

Servi​c​es

Archival Education

Provide archival instruction for individuals and institutions about best practices for physical and digital preservation.

  • One-on-one workshops
  • Small group workshops
  • Remote instruction

Collections Care

Perform and/or advise on collection care tasks for physical and digital materials including: 

  • Assessment & Selection
  • Collection & Organization
  • Preservation
  • Accessibility

Digitization

Digitally capture physical materials for preservation, ease of use, and reuse including the following formats:

  • Manuscript Materials
  • Printed Materials
  • Images
  • Audio & Video Recordings (Select Formats)
